## 📌 Overview

This repository organizes **LeetCode interview questions by company** so you can prepare with intent instead of randomly picking problems. Each company folder contains CSV datasets grouped by recency, making it easier to focus on questions that are frequently reported for interviews and online assessments.

Use it when you want to:

- 🎯 Prepare for a target company with a focused problem list.
- 🔥 Prioritize high-frequency questions before interviews.
- 🕒 Compare recent and older question trends.
- ✅ Build a personal revision tracker from clean CSV data.
- 🧭 Create company-specific DSA roadmaps for placements or job switches.

## ⚡ Quick Start

1. 🏢 Pick your target company folder.
2. 📄 Open `thirty-days.csv` or `three-months.csv` if your interview is close.
3. 🔥 Sort by `Frequency %` in descending order.
4. 🟢 Solve Easy and Medium problems first, then move to Hard.
5. 🔁 Revisit missed problems after 3, 7, and 14 days.
6. 📚 Use `all.csv` when you want complete coverage.

Recommended priority:

```text
High frequency + recent + medium difficulty
> high frequency + recent + easy
> high frequency + older
> low frequency + hard
```

You can turn this repository into a personal interview command center by adding:

Example tracker format:

```md

| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Add Two Numbers | Medium | Revise | Linked list carry handling |
```

## ✅ Best Practices

- 🎯 Do not blindly solve every problem from `all.csv`.
- 🔥 Start with recent and high-frequency problems.
- 🧠 Track mistakes, not just solved counts.
- 🔁 Re-solve important problems without looking at solutions.
- ⏱️ Practice under a timer before interviews.
- 🧩 Group problems by pattern once you have solved enough questions.
